Hyperscale Computing Core

02. HARDWARE

Neuromorphic Processing

Traditional CPUs separate memory and processing. Our proprietary "Synapse-X" chips combine them, mimicking biological neurons. This "In-Memory Computing" architecture reduces energy consumption by 90% while increasing pattern recognition speed.

These chips enable our AI to learn in real-time at the edge, allowing for autonomous decision-making in drones, robots, and IoT devices without cloud dependency.

03. SECURITY

Post-Quantum Cryptography

As quantum computers threaten standard encryption, CyberNeuro is ahead of the curve. We utilize Lattice-based cryptography to secure all data, ensuring it remains unhackable even by future quantum machines.

Our "Obsidian Protocol" generates encryption keys based on atmospheric noise and quantum fluctuations, creating truly random, unbreakable security layers.
